Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Planning Poker Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal information about its users. No accounts are required to use the Service.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. We do not run ads or sell anything.

Server Logs

Our hosting provider (Render) may automatically generate standard server logs including IP addresses and request metadata. These are retained for up to 24 months for security and troubleshooting purposes and are not used to identify individual users.

Jira Integration

If You choose to link a Jira account, We receive an OAuth access token from Atlassian. This token is used solely to fetch and update Jira issue data on Your behalf during Your active session. We do not store this token in any database or persistent storage — it exists only in server memory and is discarded when the server restarts or Your session ends.

Session Data

Planning room data (player names, votes, issues) exists only in server memory for the duration of an active session. It is never written to a database or disk and is lost when the session ends or the server restarts.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 16.

Links to Other Websites

Our Service may contain links to other websites not operated by Us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page with an updated date.
